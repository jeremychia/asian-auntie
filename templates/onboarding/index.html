Before you start

A quick word on how we handle your data.

What we collect

  • Your username and encrypted password — to identify your account
  • Your region and preferences — to personalise recipe suggestions
  • Food items and photos you add — to track your pantry and read expiry labels
  • Cooking days — to time expiry reminders

How we use it

  • To show you recipes that match what you have in your pantry
  • To send expiry reminders (only if you turn notifications on)
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

Data storage

Your data is stored on our server. We take reasonable steps to protect it. You can request deletion at any time by emailing jeremyjchia@gmail.com.

Your rights under GDPR / UK GDPR

Because you are based in the EU or UK, you have the following rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and UK GDPR:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification — ask us to correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — ask us to delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction — ask us to pause processing your data
  • Portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting past processing

Legal basis: We process your data on the basis of your consent (Article 6(1)(a) GDPR). You may withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account or emailing jeremyjchia@gmail.com.

Data controller: Jeremy Chia — jeremyjchia@gmail.com

If you have concerns about how we handle your data, you have the right to lodge a complaint with your national supervisory authority (e.g. the ICO in the UK, or your local DPA in the EU).
