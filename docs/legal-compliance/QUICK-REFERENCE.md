# Legal Compliance Quick Reference

One-page summary of legal risks and requirements for each jurisdiction.

## 🇫🇷 FRANCE

### Applicable Laws

- GDPR (EU data protection)
- French Data Protection Act
- French Copyright Law (Code de la Propriété Intellectuelle)
- French Food Code (Code Rural et de la Pêche Maritime)
- **EU Regulation 178/2002** - General Food Law (directly applicable)
- **EU Regulation 1169/2011** - Food Information to Consumers / Allergen labeling (directly applicable)
- **Digital Services Act (DSA) 2022/2065** - platform liability for online marketplaces

### Critical Requirements

| Requirement          | Action                                                           |
| -------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Privacy Policy**   | Create in French. Include lawful basis for data processing.      |
| **Consent**          | Explicit opt-in (non-pre-checked boxes) for all data collection. |
| **Cookies**          | Cookie banner with explicit consent _before_ tracking.           |
| **Recipe Copyright** | Obtain licenses for recipes. Always attribute source.            |
| **Trade ToS**        | Include food safety disclaimers and liability limits.            |

### Key Risks

- ⚠️ Processing personal data without explicit consent → GDPR fines (4% of revenue)
- ⚠️ Recipe copyright infringement → Takedown notices, legal action
- ⚠️ Facilitating unsafe food trades → Civil/criminal liability

### Contacts

- CNIL (data protection): www.cnil.fr
- Copyright Office: www.inpi.fr

## 🇩🇪 GERMANY

### Applicable Laws

- GDPR
- German Data Protection Act (BDSG)
- Telemedia Act (TMG) - **Impressum required**
- German Copyright Law (Urheberrechtsgesetz)
- **LFGB** (Lebensmittel- und Futtermittelgesetzbuch) - German Food and Feed Code
- **EU Regulation 178/2002** - General Food Law (directly applicable)
- **EU Regulation 1169/2011** - Food Information to Consumers / Allergen labeling (directly applicable)

### Critical Requirements

| Requirement             | Action                                                                        |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Impressum**           | Create /impressum page with name, address, email, phone, VAT ID. ✅ MANDATORY |
| **Privacy Policy**      | Create in German. Follow GDPR.                                                |
| **Consent**             | Explicit opt-in for all data processing.                                      |
| **Recipe Licensing**    | Obtain explicit licenses. Recipes are copyright-protected.                    |
| **Food Safety (Trade)** | Comply with LFGB (German food code). Use LFGB disclaimer.                     |
| **Content Moderation**  | Remove illegal content quickly (NetzDG).                                      |

### Key Risks

- ❌ No Impressum → Criminal offense. Fine €5,000-€50,000.
- ⚠️ GDPR violations → Fines up to €20M or 4% of revenue.
- ⚠️ Recipe copyright → Infringement liability.

### Contacts

- BDSG/GDPR: www.datenschutz.de
- Copyright (DPMA): www.dma.de

## 🇬🇧 UNITED KINGDOM

### Applicable Laws

- UK-GDPR (still applies post-Brexit)
- Data Protection Act 2018
- PECR (marketing regulation)
- Consumer Rights Act 2015
- Food Safety Act 1990
- **Food Information Regulations 2014 (SI 2014/1855)** - UK equivalent of EU 1169/2011; allergen disclosure mandatory for all 14 major allergens (Trade and Recipes)
- Food Hygiene Regulations 2006 (SI 2006/14) - applies to users handling food for others

### Critical Requirements

| Requirement             | Action                                                      |
| ----------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Privacy Notice**      | Create per UK-GDPR. State lawful basis and consumer rights. |
| **Consent**             | Explicit opt-in (no pre-checked boxes).                     |
| **Marketing**           | No marketing emails without prior explicit consent (PECR).  |
| **Data Subject Rights** | Implement access, delete, correct, portability.             |
| **Recipe Attribution**  | Obtain licenses or prove Fair Use. Always attribute.        |
| **Trade ToS**           | Include Food Safety Act warnings. Limit platform liability. |

### Key Risks

- ⚠️ Marketing without consent → Fine up to £17,500 per violation.
- ⚠️ UK-GDPR violations → ICO enforcement, fines, reputation damage.
- ⚠️ Food safety violations → Criminal liability if knowingly facilitating.

### Contacts

- ICO (Information Commissioner): www.ico.org.uk
- ASA (Advertising Standards): www.asa.org.uk

## 🇸🇬 SINGAPORE

### Applicable Laws

- Personal Data Protection Act (PDPA)
- Copyright Act
- **Sale of Food Act (Cap. 283)** - primary food safety statute (note: "FSSA" is not a standalone statute)
- **Food Regulations (Cap. 283, Rg 1)** - allergen and labeling standards (Trade and Recipes)
- **Singapore Food Agency (SFA) Act 2019** - establishes SFA as the food safety regulator
- Environmental Public Health Act (EPHA, Cap. 95) - food hygiene

### Critical Requirements

| Requirement               | Action                                                                                             |
| ------------------------- | -------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Privacy Policy**        | Create per PDPA. Specify purposes and data collection.                                             |
| **Consent**               | Explicit consent. For marketing, affirmative opt-in. Deemed consent OK for non-sensitive purposes. |
| **Data Subject Rights**   | Access, correction, erasure, withdrawal of consent.                                                |
| **Cross-border Transfer** | If storing data outside Singapore, verify destination has adequate protection.                     |
| **Recipe Licensing**      | Obtain licenses. Attribute sources clearly.                                                        |
| **Food Safety (Trade)**   | Comply with FSSA. CRITICAL: Disclose all allergens.                                                |

### Key Risks

- ⚠️ PDPA violations → Fine S$1M for individuals, higher for organizations.
- ⚠️ Allergen non-disclosure (Trade) → Criminal offense. Risk of injury claims.
- ⚠️ Recipe copyright → Infringement liability.

### Contacts

- PDPC (Personal Data Protection Commission): www.pdpc.gov.sg
- NEA (Food Safety): www.nea.gov.sg

## 🇺🇸 UNITED STATES

### Applicable Laws

- **No federal omnibus privacy law** (state-level varies)
- CCPA/CPRA (California) - applies to California residents
- VCDPA (Virginia), CPA (Colorado), etc.
- FTC Act Section 5 (deceptive practices)
- **Food Safety Modernization Act (FSMA, 21 U.S.C. §2201)** - primary federal food safety law
- **Food Allergen Labeling and Consumer Protection Act (FALCPA, 21 U.S.C. §343(w))** - 9 major allergens must be disclosed (Trade and Recipes)
- 21 CFR Part 101 - FDA food labeling regulations
- State cottage food laws - vary by state; exempt some home food trades but do not override allergen requirements

### Critical Requirements

| Requirement               | Action                                                                                         |
| ------------------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Privacy Policy**        | Create. If CA residents: CCPA compliance. Otherwise, best practice: CCPA-level rights for all. |
| **Consumer Rights**       | If CCPA: Access, delete, correct, opt-out. Recommended for all users.                          |
| **Recipe Licensing**      | Obtain licenses OR document Fair Use analysis. Always attribute.                               |
| **Food Safety (Trade)**   | Clear disclaimers. Link FDA guidance. Recommend non-perishable items.                          |
| **Claims Substantiation** | No deceptive food safety/recipe claims. Substantiate with evidence.                            |

### Key Risks

- ⚠️ CCPA violations (CA) → Fine up to $7,500 per violation + attorney fees.
- ⚠️ FTC enforcement (deceptive claims) → Cease & desist, forced compliance.
- ⚠️ Food-related harm → Personal injury claims.

### Contacts

- FTC: www.ftc.gov
- FDA: www.fda.gov
- California AG: www.oag.ca.gov

## 🇨🇦 CANADA

### Applicable Laws

- PIPEDA (federal privacy law)
- Quebec Law 25 (enhanced Quebec privacy)
- CASL (anti-spam, applies to all electronic messages)
- **Safe Food for Canadians Act (SFCA, S.C. 2012, c. 24)** - primary federal food safety statute, replacing much of the Food and Drugs Act for food safety
- **Safe Food for Canadians Regulations (SFCR, SOR/2018-108)** - Schedule 9: 14 priority allergens; mandatory disclosure
- Food and Drugs Act (R.S.C., 1985, c. F-27) - still governs adulteration and misrepresentation (§4, §5)
- CFIA (Canadian Food Inspection Agency) - enforcement body

### Critical Requirements

| Requirement             | Action                                                                                                             |
| ----------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------ |
| **Privacy Policy**      | Create in English AND French (mandatory for Quebec). Specify lawful basis.                                         |
| **Consent**             | Explicit, affirmative consent (non-pre-checked boxes). Cannot use soft opt-in.                                     |
| **CASL Compliance**     | For any emails/SMS: Opt-in consent, clear sender, unsubscribe link, mailing address. Honor unsubscribe in 10 days. |
| **Recipe Licensing**    | Obtain licenses. Attribute in English and French (Quebec).                                                         |
| **Food Safety (Trade)** | Comply with Food & Drugs Act. Follow CFIA guidelines. Allergen disclosure CRITICAL.                                |

### Key Risks

- ⚠️ CASL violation → Fine C$1M-C$15M. PER VIOLATION. ✅ HIGHEST FINE OF ALL JURISDICTIONS.
- ⚠️ PIPEDA violation → Reputational damage, forced compliance.
- ⚠️ Food & Drugs Act violation (Trade) → Criminal offense.

### Contacts

- PIPEDA: www.priv.gc.ca
- CASL: www.canada.ca/casl
- CFIA: www.inspection.canada.ca
- Health Canada: www.canada.ca/health

## 📊 Feature-Specific Summary

### Manage Perishables

**Highest Risks**: Privacy violations, data breaches
**Key Actions**: Privacy policy, consent management, encryption, data deletion

### Recommend Recipes

**Highest Risks**: Copyright infringement, deceptive claims
**Key Actions**: License recipes, attribute sources, substantiate claims, GDPR compliance

### Trade Perishables

**Highest Risks**: Food safety liability, platform liability for user actions
**Key Actions**: Clear ToS, liability disclaimers, content moderation, food safety education
