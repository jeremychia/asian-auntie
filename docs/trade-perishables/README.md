# Trade Perishables

A marketplace to trade or share perishable items with others in your community, solving the problem of excess quantities and food waste.

- **[Future Features](future-features.yaml)** — v2+ roadmap ideas (messaging, map view, ratings, payments, etc.)
- **[Open Questions](questions.yaml)** — Decisions still in progress and unknowns

## At a Glance

| Aspect | Status |
|--------|--------|
| Primary User | Single/small household cooks with excess ingredients |
| Scope | Local marketplace to trade or share perishables |
| Trade Models | Give away, trade 1:1, or group buy together |
| MVP Focus | List excess items, browse local offerings |
| Phase | Design & specification |
