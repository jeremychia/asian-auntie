# Recommend Recipe

## Problem Statement

You have ingredients left in your fridge.
You want to know what you can cook - which is Asian, and backed by other people's recipes.
You want to make sure these are grounded in actual recipes, and not something random which LLMs suggested.

## How to solve?

1. Define ingredients available
2. Search for recipes
3. Ask for inputs
4. Make suggestions

## Details

### Ingredients

Phase 1: User keys in the ingredients which are avaible
Phase 2: Auto-recognition of ingredients

## Recipes

Phase 1: Take recipes from rasamalaysia (create a repository of it)
Phase 2: Automatically search 

Note: recipes recommended should always have source described.

## Reinforcement learning

When a recipe is recommended, have a say or indicating if the recipe is good or not

## Reviews

Allow people to take pictures of what they've created
Create a community for it.
